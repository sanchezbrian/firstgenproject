• Tips for Grade Nine

Grade 9 is an important foundational year. This is when your GPA will start being calculated and depending on what you want/ need at the end of high school, you want to maintain that GPA at a certain number. Your grades here will weigh into your GPA and/or class rank, so you want to work hard. Ultimately, your grades in Grade 9 will not make or break you, but it's still in your best interest to look out for yourself.
