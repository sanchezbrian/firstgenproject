• Tips for Grade Nine

Grade 9 is an important foundational year. This is when your GPA will start being calculated and depending on what you want/ need at the end of high school, you want to maintain that GPA at a certain number. Your grades here will weigh into your GPA and/or class rank, so you want to work hard. Ultimately, your grades in Grade 9 will not make or break you, but it's still in your best interest to look out for yourself.


During Grade 9, you'll have the option to take Regular versus Honours classes (and depending on your school possibly a few AP classes). Why take an honours class instead of a regular class? There are different reasons as to why you would. For starters, do you want to challenge yourself or are you interested in staying in your comfort zone?


You don't need to be too involved in your first year. It's just important that you stay focused in school, and start developing good study habits.
